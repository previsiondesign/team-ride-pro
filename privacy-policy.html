Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Tamalpais High School Mountain Bike Team Practice Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our team practice management system.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information to manage team practices and communicate with team members:

  • Name: To identify team members and assign practice groups
  • Phone Number: To send verification codes for account access and important team communications
  • Email Address: For account verification and team communications
  • Team Information: Grade level, racing group, fitness level, and other relevant team data
  • Medical Information: Allergies and medical conditions (voluntarily provided) for safety during practices

2.2 How We Collect Information

Information is collected through:

  • Team registration forms
  • Direct input by team coaches and administrators
  • CSV imports from official team rosters
  • User-provided information during account setup

3. SMS/Text Message Consent

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS/text messages from us for the following purposes:

  • Verification codes for account access and login authentication
  • Important team practice updates and notifications
  • Emergency communications related to team activities

Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any message or by contacting us directly. However, opting out of SMS messages may limit your ability to access certain features of the system that require verification.

Frequency: Messages are sent on an as-needed basis, typically when you request account access or when important team information needs to be communicated.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Manage team practice schedules and group assignments
  • Authenticate your identity when accessing your account
  • Send verification codes for secure login
  • Coordinate practice attendance and coach assignments
  • Ensure safety during practices (medical information)
  • Communicate important team updates and information
  • Generate practice reports and attendance records

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

Your information is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud-based database service that complies with industry-standard security practices. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication requiring verification codes
  • Role-based access control limiting who can view and edit information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Team Coaches and Administrators: Authorized team coaches and administrators have access to team member information necessary for managing practices
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (Supabase for data storage, Twilio for SMS delivery) that process your information under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of team members
  • School Administration: Information may be shared with Tamalpais High School administration as required for school-affiliated activities

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to school record-keeping requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of SMS messages by replying "STOP" or contacting us
  • Account Deactivation: Request deactivation of your account

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

This system is used by high school students (typically ages 14-18). We comply with applicable privacy laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Parental consent may be required for students under 13, and we work with school administration to ensure compliance.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are an active team member and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes. Medical information is retained only as long as necessary for safety during team activities. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to school record-keeping requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the system after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Terms of Service

11.1 Acceptable Use

By using this system, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Use the system only for authorized team-related purposes
  • Not share your login credentials with others
  • Respect the privacy of other team members
  • Follow all school and team policies

11.2 Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account. You agree to notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account.

11.3 System Availability

We strive to maintain system availability but do not guarantee uninterrupted access. The system may be temporarily unavailable for maintenance, updates, or due to circumstances beyond our control.
